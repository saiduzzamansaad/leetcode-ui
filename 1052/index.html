How It Works

This tool helps bookstore owners maximize customer satisfaction by strategically applying an anti-grumpiness technique.

Input: Enter the number of customers per minute and mark which minutes the owner is grumpy.

Technique: The owner can stay non-grumpy for a continuous window of minutes (your input).

Output: The tool finds the optimal window to apply the technique to maximize satisfied customers.
